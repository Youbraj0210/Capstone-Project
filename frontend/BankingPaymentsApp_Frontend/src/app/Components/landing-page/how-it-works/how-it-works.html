How It Works

Our Corporate Banking Payment System makes client management, payments, and salary disbursement easy and secure.

01

Customer Onboarding

Bank users onboard new clients by entering their details and uploading required documents for verification.

02

Process Payments

Client users can initiate payments to beneficiaries, which are approved or rejected securely by bank users.

03

Disburse Salaries

Clients can disburse salaries to employees individually or in batches, with automated notifications and receipts.
