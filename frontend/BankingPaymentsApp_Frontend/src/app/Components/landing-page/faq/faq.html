Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our Corporate Banking Payment Application.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, the system uses bank-level encryption, secure OAuth 2.0 authentication, and multi-factor verification to ensure your data is fully protected.

Who can access the system?

There are three user roles: Super Admin, Bank User, and Client User. Each role has specific permissions for managing banks, clients, payments, and reports.

How do I process payments?

Client Users can initiate payments to beneficiaries or employees, which Bank Users can then approve or reject. Salary disbursement is supported individually or in batch.

Can I generate reports?

Yes, all users can generate reports based on their roles, including transaction reports, payment reports, and salary disbursement reports.

How do I upload documents?

Bank and Client Users can securely upload required documents for customer verification or transaction purposes, with role-based access control.
